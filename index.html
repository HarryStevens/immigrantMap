Who wants to be an American?

These maps show the country of origin of immigrants who obtained legal permanent resident status in 2012. Mexicans lead the way, as 146,406 of them obtained legal permanent resident status in the United States in 2012. Other major contributors are China (81,784), India (66,434) and the Philippines (57,327).
Use the box to the left to explore the data by geographic region. Drag your cursor over a country to see how many new legal permanent residents (LPRs) came from there. SOURCE: U.S. Department of Homeland Security immigration statistics
